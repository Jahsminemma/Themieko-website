Goals for social media marketing

  • Know your targeted audience

    A lack of knowledge about targeted audience will restrict your business from growing

  • Where your target audience is

    You need to know what platform your customers use the most and leverage on that more. If your customers are fans of Facebook, concentrate on that don’t divert because you want to have various social media handles.

  • What message do you intend to pass to your audience

    The message must be precise and clear, don’t pass the wrong message to your audience because you need to build enough trust. Be careful of what you intend to share.

  • What do you intend to achieve

    What do you intend to achieve, is your brand made to sell and educate your audience? Be specific about what you intend to achieve.
